Yiqun T. Chen

Stanford Data Science + DBDS; Statistical Insight for Data Science.

I am Yiqun Chen (pronunciation here), currently a Data Science Postdoctoral Fellow at Stanford University, working with Professor James Zou on applying advanced AI techniques to biomedical data. Previously, I completed my PhD in Biostatistics under the guidance of Professor Daniela Witten at the University of Washington, where I developed methods to test data-driven hypotheses. My application interest spans different fields of data science: from dermatology, social networks and epidemiology, as well as human-computer interaction and microbiome science.
